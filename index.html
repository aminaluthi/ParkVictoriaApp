Help & Support

Frequently asked questions

How do I find my space number?
Your space number is displayed on the sign next to or in front of your parking spot. It is usually a 4-digit number painted on the ground or on a nearby post.
What if I go overtime?
If you exceed your session time, parking officers may issue a fine. You can extend your session from the Active Session screen before it expires.
Can I extend my session?
Yes — tap "Extend Session" from the Active Session screen. Extensions are subject to the maximum stay limit for your zone.
How do refunds work?
If you end your session early, unused time is automatically refunded to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

We collect location data during parking sessions, payment information, and app usage analytics. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

How We Use It

Your data is used solely to provide parking services, process payments, and improve app functionality. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

Session history is retained for 12 months. Payment data is stored with our PCI-compliant payment processor. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact privacy@parkvictoria.ca for any requests.

Cookies

This app uses local storage for session management only. No third-party tracking cookies are used.
